About us

Elite Media Group is a media group which was founded on 20th February 2025 by group of Journalism and Mass Communication students of Chuka University. Elite Media Group emerged as the founder students were eager to start and run a media house worldwide.
Elite Media has been a cornerstone for Journalism and Media students countrywide as we have helped other students to practically put into action what they have learnt.
As Elite Media Group we look forward to foster credibility among the journalism practitioners and inform the community timely.

Mission

To foster credibility in Journalism and inform community timely.

Vision

To be the most reliable and trusted media group.

What we do

As Elite Media Group we carry various journalism activities such as videography, photography, writing, news reporting and anchoring.
We also offer spaces for talents exploration where by the students comes out and express their talents.
